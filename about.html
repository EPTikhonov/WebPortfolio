About me


I'm an up-and-coming software engineer in Green Bay, WI. When I'm not working on school assignments, I enjoy programming my own projects and customizing an operating system called Linux. Other activities include reading, tennis, and biking. Learn more about each hobby and/or activity below!


Main Hobbies

Customizing Linux

I spend a few hours a week customizing the look and feel of a highly customizable Linux distribution called Arch Linux. Most of my customizations involve small scripts, configuration files, and packages that help me learn Linux and becoming a power user. One major package group I use is i3 to increase overall performance. I also use Ubuntu for school because of stability reasons.
